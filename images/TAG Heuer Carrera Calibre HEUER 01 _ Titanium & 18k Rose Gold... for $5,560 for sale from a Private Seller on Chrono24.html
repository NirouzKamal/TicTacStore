Description
The TAG Heuer Carrera Calibre HEUER 01 in titanium with 18k rose gold accents delivers a bold, modern expression of the iconic Carrera design. Originally retailing at $10,850, this model offers exceptional value for collectors seeking a high-impact, contemporary sports chronograph at nearly half the original cost.

Its skeletonized black dial exposes the mechanical architecture of the HEUER 01 movement, giving the watch a striking technical presence. The lightweight titanium case, complemented by 18k rose gold PVD details, enhances durability while elevating the aesthetic with a luxurious two-tone contrast. The fixed black ceramic tachymeter bezel adds further scratch resistance and completes the sporty-luxury look.

Paired with a comfortable black perforated rubber strap, this 2018 example is in pre-owned condition with standard signs of careful wear. Offered as a complete box and papers set, it represents a standout choice for enthusiasts seeking a modern Carrera with advanced materials and strong wrist presence.
